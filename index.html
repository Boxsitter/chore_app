About The App

ChoreManager is designed to simplify the way you and your roommates or family members manage household tasks. Our intuitive interface makes it easy to assign chores, track progress, and ensure fairness. No more arguments over whose turn it is to clean the bathroom or take out the trash!

This project showcases a clean, user-friendly design focused on clarity and ease of use, demonstrating key concepts in web application development and UI/UX principles.

Key Features

  • Chore Assignment: Easily assign tasks to household members with due dates.
  • Progress Tracking: See who has completed their chores and what's still pending.
  • Rotating Schedules: Set up recurring chores that rotate automatically.
  • Notifications & Reminders: (Future Feature) Get alerts for upcoming or overdue tasks.
  • Points & Rewards System: (Future Feature) Gamify chores by awarding points for completion.
  • Shared Calendar: View all chores and deadlines in a shared household calendar.
  • Polls & Decisions: Create polls to decide on shared expenses, house rules, or group activities (see our Poll Template!).
