Welcome to Choreboard!

The easiest way to organize household chores and keep everyone accountable.

About The App

Choreboard is designed to simplify the way you and your roommates or family members manage household tasks. Our intuitive interface makes it easy to assign chores, track progress, and ensure fairness. No more arguments over whose turn it is to clean the bathroom or take out the trash!

This project showcases a clean, user-friendly design focused on clarity and ease of use, demonstrating key concepts in web application development and UI/UX design.

Key Features

  • Chore Assignment & Management:
    • Easily assign tasks to household members with due dates.
    • Set priority levels for chores (e.g., High, Medium, Low).
    • Add detailed descriptions, instructions, or checklists to chores.
    • Ability for users to claim available/unassigned chores.
    • Option to mark chores as "up for grabs" or "needs approval" before completion.
  • Progress Tracking & Visibility:
    • See who has completed their chores and what's still pending.
    • Visual progress bars or completion percentages for individuals and the household.
    • Chore history log to see past completions and assignments.
  • Flexible Scheduling & Automation:
    • Set up recurring chores (daily, weekly, monthly, custom intervals).
    • Rotating Schedules: Automatically rotate chore assignments among members.
      • Define rotation order and frequency.
      • Option to skip a turn or temporarily pause participation.
    • Schedule one-time chores for specific events or needs.
    • "Round Robin" assignment mode for fair distribution.
  • Notifications & Reminders (Future Feature):
    • Get personalized alerts for upcoming or overdue tasks.
    • In-app notifications and optional email/push notifications.
    • Reminders for unassigned critical chores.
    • Notifications for when a chore is marked complete or needs review.
  • Communication & Collaboration:
    • Comment section for specific chores.
    • Shared household notes or bulletin board.
  • Shared Calendar & Overview:
    • View all chores, deadlines, and assignments in a shared calendar.
    • Filter calendar by roommate, chore status, or date.
    • Integration with personal calendars (e.g., Google Calendar, etc.).
  • Fairness & Accountability:
    • Track chore completion consistency.
    • Mechanisms for verifying chore completion (e.g., photo upload).
    • Shame your roommates with an anonymous public photo of their mess if they don't complete their task.
  • Customization & Personalization:
    • Customizable chore categories or tags.
    • Theme options or color personalization for the app interface (Future Feature).
    • Ability to define "rooms" or "areas" and assign chores accordingly.
